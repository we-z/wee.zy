Wheezy

Choose what chooses you. Too often, we try to force love, relationships, or friendships from people who aren’t right for us, not realizing that we deserve genuine connections. The world is full of blessings within our reach, but we sometimes think they are too good for us. It’s essential to understand our worth and stop being our biggest blockers to getting what we want, even if it seems totally out of reach.

Loving yourself is a key part of this journey. Accepting who you are, flaws and all, is the foundation for opening up to the abundance life offers. This includes letting in love, even when it feels scary, and believing you deserve the kindness and care that come your way. Along the way, it’s crucial to quickly spot those who are hurting and judge themselves harshly, as they might try to hurt and judge you too. Choosing self-peace means staying away from that negative energy as much as possible.

By valuing ourselves and being mindful of the company we keep, we can build healthier, more fulfilling relationships that truly support our well-being. Stepping out of our comfort zones and embracing the amazing opportunities life presents allows us to see and feel all the good stuff out there for us. In doing so, we move closer to a life filled with genuine love and peace, recognizing that we are worthy of all the wonderful things the world has to offer.
